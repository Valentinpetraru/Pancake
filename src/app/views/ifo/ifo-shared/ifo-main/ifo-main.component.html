How to Take Part in the Public Sale

1

Active your Profile

You'll need an active PancekaSwap Profile to take part in an IFO

2

Stake CAKE in IFO pool

The maximum amount of CAKE user can commit to the Public Sale, is equal to the average CAKE balance in the IFO CAKE pool prior to the IFO. Stake more CAKE to increase the maximum CAKE you can commit to the sale. Missed this IFO? You can keep staking in the IFO CAKE Pool to join the next IFO sale.

How does the IFO credit calculated?

IFO credit is calculated by avarage block balance in the IFO pool in over the staking period announced with each IFO proposal. Please refer to out blog post for more details.

3

Commit CAKE

When the IFO sales are live, you can “commit” your CAKE to buy the tokens being sold.

4

Claim your tokens and achievement

After the IFO sales finish, you can claim any IFO tokens that you bought, and any unspent CAKE tokens will be returned to your wallet.
